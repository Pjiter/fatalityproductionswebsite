2016: European Championship, Will grigg's on fire!

The european soccer championship was well underway. There was 1 Popular song: Will Grigg's on fire. He is a soccer player and everybody sang his song. We uploaded this song and it was our first viral video with more than 2000 views every day. sadly our happines didn't last long because the video got striked and our channel had almost no publicity for 3 months.

31 May 2016: Founding of Fatality Productions

I had the idea to start a music channel to make audio spectrums. I uploaded a couple of videos and only got 50 views per day. I stopped for a while and asked a friend of mine if he would like to co-own the channel. This worked and the channel grew exponentially.
